# Lovable prompt — Dr Sana Amraoui website

Build a **fast, mobile-first, French-language website** for a Parisian cardiologist. You have full freedom over the layout, structure, sections, typography, and overall look — design it as you see best. The only design direction: choose a **colour palette grounded in medical colour psychology** — colours that build trust and calm an anxious cardiac patient while reading as authority, warmth, and precision. Avoid clichés (generic healthcare-blue gradients and literal red bleeding-hearts). I'm giving you the client, the audience, the goals, and the content — not how it should look.

## Client
**Dr Sana Amraoui** — Cardiologue & Rythmologue interventionnelle (she treats the heart's *electrical* system). Cheffe d'unité, Hôpital Américain de Paris; founder & CEO of Cardio Check-Up (prevention centre); LSE MBA in health economics; trained at Columbia (NYC), St Thomas' (London), Bordeaux; all diplomas earned with highest honours. Specialities: atrial fibrillation, catheter ablation (incl. cutting-edge electroporation), pacemakers/defibrillators, the sleep-apnea↔heart link, connected watches.

## Audience
1. **Anxious patients** — French-speaking, often scared (palpitations), mostly on mobile, sometimes at 2am. They need reassurance, instant credibility, and an easy way to book.
2. **Peers, referrers, journalists, international patients** (French + English) — they need authority and depth.

## Goals
- Feel like a world-class Parisian specialist, not a generic clinic.
- Convert anxious visitors into booked appointments (booking via Doctolib; phone matters too).
- Be citable by AI search engines (ChatGPT / Perplexity / Google AI).

## Content to include (data, not layout)
- **Identity:** Dr Sana Amraoui · Rythmologue interventionnelle · Cheffe d'unité, Hôpital Américain de Paris. She describes a rythmologue as *"the electrician of the heart."* Primary action: **Prendre rendez-vous** + phone.
- **Authority (no patient reviews exist — use these instead):** institutions (Hôpital Américain de Paris · LSE · St Thomas' · Columbia · Bordeaux) and media/peer recognition (*Le Cardiologue* "Portrait du mois", EHRA 2026 congress, Radcliffe Cardiology author, PariSanté Campus, YouTube @drsanaamraoui).
- **Conditions:** Fibrillation atriale (world's most common arrhythmia — 45M worldwide, ~1M in France, 230 000 new cases/year, ×4.8 stroke risk, 1 in 4 adults over 40) · palpitations / tachycardie & bradycardie · sleep apnea (~2M in France, mostly undiagnosed, frequent cause of FA).
- **Treatments:** catheter ablation (radiofrequency / cryoablation / electroporation, often curative) · pacemakers & defibrillators · connected health & remote monitoring (real case: a 190 bpm tachycardia caught by a smartwatch, then cured).
- **Prevention:** Cardio Check-Up (personalised cardiovascular check-ups).
- **Education:** a blog with patient guides + actualités, organised in five categories — Fibrillation & Arythmies, Traitements & Interventions, Examens & Diagnostic, Apnée & Sommeil, Actualités.
- **FAQ:** e.g. cardiologue vs rythmologue? · when to consult? · is a referral letter needed?
- **Contact:** Doctolib booking (link: `https://www.doctolib.fr/cardiologue/neuilly-sur-seine/sana-amraoui`) · phone (tap-to-call) · email · two locations (Hôpital Américain de Paris / Neuilly · cabinet in Paris) · note she is secteur 2 (honoraires libres).
- Use neat labelled placeholder blocks where photos will go (no stock images).

## Requirements
French-first (light English microcopy is fine), mobile-first, fast Core Web Vitals, accessible (keyboard navigation, visible focus, skip-to-content), and SEO + JSON-LD structured data (Physician, FAQPage) so AI engines can cite her. Calm, institutional French tone — short CTA labels, no aggressive sales copy. Booking is a Doctolib link (no in-page calendar). Don't include a star-rating/reviews widget (none exist yet).
